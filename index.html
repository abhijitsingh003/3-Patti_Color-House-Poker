Hand Rankings

  • 1. Trail (Three of a Kind) Three cards of the exact same rank (like three Aces). This is the rarest and most powerful hand you can get.
    Ex: A♠ A♥ A♣ (Best), 2♠ 2♦ 2♣ (Lowest Trail)
  • 2. Pure Sequence (Straight Flush) Three consecutive cards that belong to the same color house (Suit). They must match in color and order.
    Ex: A♥ K♥ Q♥ (Highest), 4♠ 3♠ 2♠ (Lowest)
  • 3. Sequence (Straight) Three consecutive cards (like 4, 5, 6). You can put cards from different color houses (Suits) here.
    Ex: A♠ K♦ Q♣ (Highest), 5♥ 4♠ 3♦ (Lowest)
  • 4. Color (Flush) All three cards belong to the same color house (e.g., all Hearts), but they are not in a sequential order.
    Ex: A♦ K♦ J♦ (High Card matters), 2♠ 5♠ 9♠
  • 5. Pair (Two of a Kind) Two cards hold the same rank (e.g., two Kings). If two players have a pair, the higher pair wins.
    Ex: A♠ A♣ K♥ (Beats A-A-J), 8♦ 8♠ J♣
  • 6. High Card If you have none of the above, your highest single card determines your strength against others.
    Ex: A♠ (Wins against K, Q, J, etc.)

Goal: Get the best hand possible!
